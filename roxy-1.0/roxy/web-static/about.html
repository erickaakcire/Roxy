About Roxy
Roxy was developed with respect for privacy and fully informed consent as a primary goal. Ease of use on different computers and devices, as well as transparency and user-control also guided the development of the software for the researcher Ericka Menchen-Trevino by Audacious Software LLC.

Roxy was named as a blend of the words research and proxy. Roxy is a proxy server, a very common technology that many businesses use. The Roxy software runs on a server on the Internet, not on users' computers. Users simply configure their computer to connect to Roxy and do not need to install any software.
